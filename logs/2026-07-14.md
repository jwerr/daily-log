# 2026-07-14

Topic focus: Transformer architecture

What I worked on: Studied the fundamentals of transformer architecture, covering embeddings, positional encoding, self-attention (Query, Key, Value), multi-head attention, feed-forward layers, and the encoder-decoder vs decoder-only structure.

What I learned: Self-attention lets each token weigh every other token in the sequence directly, which is why transformers handle long-range dependencies better than RNNs. Multi-head attention runs several attention computations in parallel to capture different types of relationships between words.

References:
Attention Is All You Need (Vaswani et al., 2017): https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.03762
